My way into Muay Thai

Hello everyone, my name is Leon. I started with Muay Thai when I was seventeen years old. At that point, competitive sport has always been my greatest passion. After one year of training, I decided to compete and won my first amateur fight. Within the following two years, I fought four more fights. After a two year break during Covid19, I decided to follow my dream and gave my Muay Thai debut under professional rules in a local stadium in Thailand, against a fighter who was 10 kg heavier and much more experienced (100+ professional fights).
