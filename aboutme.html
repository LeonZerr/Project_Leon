My way into Muay Thai

Hello everyone, my name is Leon. I started to train when I was seventeen years old. Eventough I was completly unexpierienced, I instantly falled in love with the training and philosophy of the sport. After one year of training, I decided to compete and won my first amateur-fight. Within the following two years, I fought four more fights. After a two year break during Covid19, I decided to follow my dream. In 2022 I traveled to Thailand and successfully gave my Muay Thai debut under professional rules in a local stadium, against an experienced fighter (100+ fights) who was 10kg heavier.
