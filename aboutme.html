About myself

(Fight Record: 4 wins, 3 losses, 1 draw)

Hello everyone, my name is Leon. I started to train when I was seventeen years old. Eventough I was completly unexpierienced, I instantly falled in love with the training and philosophy of the sport. After one year of training, I decided to compete and won my first amateur-fight. Within the following two years, I fought four more fights. After a two year break during Covid19, I decided to follow my dream. In 2022 I traveled to Thailand and successfully gave my Muay Thai debut under professional rules in a local stadium, against an experienced Fighter (100+ fights) who was 10kg heavier. (video below)

Six months later I started coaching. Until today, I share my knowledge to everyone who wanna learn 'The Art of Eight Limbs'. All levels are welcome - from beginners to professionals! It doesn't matter whether you wanna fight (amateur to professional) or just wanna gain a healty lifestyle and enjoy learning new skills. Together we can reach your goals!

I am located in Leverkusen (Germany). You can book private or group sessions. Online training is also available. If you are interested, feel free to contact us via our contact form. See you soon!
